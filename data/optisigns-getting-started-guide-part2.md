## **Upload Your Content**

Go to [**Files/Assets**](https://app.optisigns.com/app/assetManagement) (Assets in OptiSigns 2.0) page → **Upload Files/Add New** → **Upload Files (2.0 only)****.**

Then **drop** , **import** , or **browse** for files to upload to your account.

## **Push Content to Your Screen**

Go back to the **Screens** page, and click **Edit** on your preferred screen (on 2.0, simply click the **screen's name**).

**OptiSigns 1.0** | **OptiSigns 2.0**  
---|---  
![](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/46546449262867) | ![](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/46546421120147)  
  
For **Type** , select your desired content: asset, playlist or schedule. For this example, we chose an **Asset.**

Then, select your desired content from the drop down menu.
