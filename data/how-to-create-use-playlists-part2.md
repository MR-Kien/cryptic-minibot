**7.** Lastly, when your Playlist is ready, you can push your content to screens by selecting the '**Push to Screens** ' icon on the top of your playlist.

**OptiSigns 1.0** | **OptiSigns 2.0**  
---|---  
![](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/28600828704787) | ![](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/46546231897619)  
  
You can:

  * Push your content to a screen(s) immediately

  * Schedule your content for a future time

  * Temporarily take over the screen for a specific period (i.e. 5 minutes), then revert back to previous content.
